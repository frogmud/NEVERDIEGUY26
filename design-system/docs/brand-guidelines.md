# Brand Guidelines

Official brand rules for NEVER DIE GUY.

## Brand Name

**NEVER DIE GUY** is the trademarked brand name.

- Always written in all caps: NEVER DIE GUY
- The brand name also serves as the tagline
- No separate taglines (removed: "data is eternal", "death is essential")

## Logo

Primary logo: `brand/logos/ndg-skull-dome.svg`

### Usage Rules
- Maintain clear space around logo
- Do not distort, rotate, or add effects
- Use on dark backgrounds only
- Minimum size: 32px

## Color

Dark mode only aesthetic.

- Primary: `#E90441` (pinky red)
- Secondary: `#00e5ff` (cyan)
- Background: `#0a0a0a`

See `docs/colors.md` for full palette.

## Typography

- Primary: Inter
- Gaming: m6x11plus (pixel font)

See `docs/typography.md` for scale.

## Iconography

- Use MUI icons with `*Sharp` variants
- No emojis in assets or documentation
- Consistent stroke weight

## Voice & Tone

- Direct, no-nonsense
- Gaming-focused vocabulary
- Dark humor acceptable
- No excessive punctuation

## Don'ts

- No emojis in code, docs, or assets
- No light mode designs
- No separate taglines
- No distortion of logo
- No Comic Sans
