# Voice checklist (gate every line)

Distilled from `../docs/skills/NDG_CONSOLIDATED_WRITING_SKILL.md`. Read the full skill for the
clarity -> tone -> aphorism passes; this is the fast gate.

## Vocabulary (use cleanly, do not over-bone)
- Bones = what the player throws. Faces = what answers. Graveyard = buried + still active.
- Archive = what can be proven. Myth = what people say. Favor = who noticed.
- Corruption = what came back wrong.
- Do not append "bone" to every noun. Do not foreground the old acronym.

## Hard style rules
- No em dashes. No emojis. Sentence case. Active voice. Minimal punctuation.
- "Throw the Bones. Feed the Myth." is a banner - at most once per pack, never as seasoning.
- Keep the facts, cut the fog. Shorter wins. HUD lines must fit a short bubble.

## Tone by beat
- Threat without splatter; the game is not reveal-and-die.
- Death is a change in custody, not a reset or a taunt about losing.
- Faces are things with their own jurisdiction, not generic monsters.

## Myth drift, same facts
- low: plain and local. mid: references accrue. high: mythologized framing.
- Never change what happened across tiers - only how the world frames it.

## Reject a line if
- it uses an emoji or em dash, or repeats the banner,
- it reads as dice-first instead of Bones/Faces,
- it gloats about death-as-loss,
- it is longer than the HUD can show,
- it invents a mechanic the schema does not have.
